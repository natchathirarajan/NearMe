About the Sri kattu veera Temple

The temple is dedicated to Lord Hanuman, a revered deity in Hinduism known for his unwavering devotion to Lord Rama. The history and origins of the temple may vary, but many such temples dedicated to Lord Hanuman are often associated with ancient legends and stories of divine intervention. Devotees visit the temple to seek the blessings of Lord Hanuman for protection, strength, courage, and success in their endeavors..
